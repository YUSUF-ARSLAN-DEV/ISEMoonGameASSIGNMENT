Jump height in pixels:
height = (velocity²) / (2 × gravity)
Time in air (frames):
frames = (2 × velocity) / gravity
So if you want to jump exactly 100px high at gravity = 0.5:
velocity = √(2 × 0.5 × 100) = √100 = 10
